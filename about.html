About Us

Petal & Thorn Tattoos is an up-and-coming tattoo shop located in downtown Eau Claire, Wisconsin. We specialize in American Traditional tattooing and are passionate about creating unique and exciting tattoo designs for every customer. Our experienced professionals offer a wide variety of body piercings, including but not limited to lip, ear, bridge, and nose piercings. Petal & Thorn also offers “Flash Friday,” where clients can walk in and choose from a variety of flash tattoos, all for $50 and done that day (first come, first serve policy).
